Ongoing Support

We turn mistakes from routine firefighting to consistently great customer service. We provide excellent visibility and centralized control when dealing with issues to ensure there is no downtime on the web. We have been bringing smiles to many pages, end users and stakeholders. Websites need ongoing support to get the best results. Building a website is only the start of a journey.

Great way to get traffic and build an audience, they don't last forever. In order to keep your website fresh and give your users the best possible experience, you need ongoing support. Ongoing support means regularly updating content, whether that's fixing broken links or creating new blog posts. This means continuous improvement of the website.

Part of the ongoing support we provide involves monitoring how your users interact with your web content. This includes analyzing which pages are getting the most attention, what content is converting into leads or sales, and which pages users are spending more or less time on. This means we can tweak the content, design and overall user experience as needed.
